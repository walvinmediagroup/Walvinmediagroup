CREATE A COMPLETE, PROFESSIONAL WEBSITE FOR A SHEFFIELD-BASED SOCIAL MEDIA MANAGEMENT BUSINESS

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Create a polished, modern, conversion-focused website for a solo social media management business based in Sheffield, United Kingdom.

Business name: [BUSINESS NAME]
Founder name: [FOUNDER NAME]
Business email: [EMAIL ADDRESS]
Telephone number: [PHONE NUMBER]
Instagram: [INSTAGRAM URL]
LinkedIn: [LINKEDIN URL]
Booking link: [CALENDLY OR BOOKING URL]
Primary location: Sheffield, South Yorkshire
Service area: Sheffield and surrounding areas, with remote services available throughout the UK

The business helps small and medium-sized businesses improve their social media presence through content planning, content creation, posting, community management, strategy and reporting.

The initial target customers are:

- Independent cafés and restaurants
- Barbers, salons and beauty businesses
- Tradespeople and home-service businesses
- Gyms and fitness businesses
- Independent retailers
- Professional service businesses
- Other small businesses in Sheffield

The website should present the business as personal, affordable and approachable, while still looking organised, strategic and professional.

The desired positioning is:

“The personal attention of a freelancer, delivered with the organisation and professionalism of an agency.”

Do not make the business appear to be a large agency with a large team. Be transparent that clients work directly with the founder.

Do not invent clients, testimonials, awards, statistics, accreditations or campaign results. Any demonstration work must be clearly labelled as sample work or concept work.

PRIMARY WEBSITE GOALS

The website should:

1. Generate enquiries from Sheffield small businesses.
2. Encourage visitors to request a free social media audit.
3. Encourage visitors to book an introductory call.
4. Clearly explain what social media management includes.
5. Show sample content and strategic thinking.
6. Build trust in a new business without using fabricated results.
7. Rank for relevant local searches such as:
   - Social media management Sheffield
   - Social media manager Sheffield
   - Social media agency Sheffield
   - Content creation Sheffield
   - Instagram management Sheffield
   - Social media services for small businesses Sheffield

DESIGN STYLE

Create a modern, confident and energetic design inspired by contemporary social media agencies, but make it accessible and appropriate for local small businesses.

The design should feel:

- Modern
- Creative
- Friendly
- Clear
- Trustworthy
- Energetic
- Local
- Personal
- Professional
- Not overly corporate
- Not childish
- Not excessively futuristic

Avoid generic corporate website design.

Avoid excessive gradients, glassmorphism, neon effects, stock photographs of office meetings and generic images of people pointing at charts.

Use generous spacing, strong typography, clear hierarchy and visually engaging examples of social content.

The website should combine:

- Bold editorial-style headings
- Clean layouts
- Rounded content cards
- Social media post mock-ups
- Phone-screen mock-ups
- Subtle motion
- Realistic imagery of local businesses
- Sheffield references used subtly

Do not rely on obvious Sheffield clichés throughout the site. Sheffield landmarks can appear in one or two areas, but the design should not be covered in skyline illustrations.

- Synergy

RESPONSIVE DESIGN

The website must be fully responsive.

Desktop:
- Maximum content width around 1,200–1,320px
- Generous whitespace
- Multi-column layouts
- Large typography

Tablet:
- Reduce oversized typography
- Convert complex three-column sections into two columns
- Maintain comfortable tap targets

Mobile:
- Single-column layouts
- Minimum 16px body text
- Minimum 44px tap targets
- Full-width primary buttons where appropriate
- No horizontal scrolling
- No content hidden solely because of screen size
- Images compressed and correctly cropped
- Cards stacked in a logical order
- Sticky mobile call-to-action may appear after the visitor scrolls

FINAL DESIGN PRINCIPLES

Prioritise clarity over decoration.

The visitor should understand within five seconds:

- What the business does
- Who it helps
- Where it is based
- Why it is useful
- What action they should take

Use the Sheffield location naturally for local relevance, but make it clear that remote clients across the UK are also welcome.

The site should look credible for a new founder-led business without pretending the business is larger or more established than it currently is.

Make the calls to action highly visible, but do not make the site feel aggressive.

The final result should feel like a modern social media agency website adapted specifically for Sheffield small businesses: bold enough to demonstrate creativity, clear enough to build trust and simple enough to generate enquiries.
